# Watch Out! For Cars

Watch Out! For Cars is my 2016 video game for Samsung Gear S2 and other Samsung smart watches on Tizen OS. 
Its gameplay is inspired by a 1974 arcade hit _[Speed Race](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5jc9_xrD2_M)_: 
you are driving a speeding car and trying to evade traffic for as long as possible by rotating the watch's besel.

https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1326899c-1bdd-41c0-8cab-9bd04ee6ce1a

Due to Tizen App Store shutdown in 2025, the game is not available to download anymore.

## Tech stack

Tizen SDK, HTML, JavaScript.
